1. Sirius: Context & Concepts 

Sirius in a nutshell

An Eclipse-based tooling to define specific multi-view workbenches.


2 main components:

  • Specification environment to
    • Define custom modeling editors (Diagrams, Trees, Tables...)
    • Test and distribute graphical modeling workbenches
    • With little technical knowledge

  • The associated runtime
